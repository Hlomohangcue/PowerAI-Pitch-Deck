## ğŸ¨ Canva Design Tips

### Color Palette
Use these hex codes in Canva:
- **Primary Green:** #7fb069
- **Dark Green:** #1e3c28  
- **Medium Green:** #2c5530
- **Light Green:** #a8d5ba
- **White:** #FFFFFF
- **Text:** #FFFFFF or #1e3c28

### Fonts Recommendations
- **Headings:** Montserrat Bold, Poppins Bold, or Raleway Bold
- **Body Text:** Open Sans, Roboto, or Lato
- **Numbers:** Montserrat Extra Bold for statistics

### Icons
Search in Canva for:
- Energy icons (lightning, solar panel, wind turbine)
- Technology icons (robot, chart, dashboard)
- Business icons (money, growth, team)
- Location icons (Africa map, globe)

### Images (Stock Photos)
Search Canva for:
- "Solar panels africa"
- "Wind energy"
- "Technology dashboard"
- "Business team diverse"
- "Green energy sustainable"

### Layout Tips
1. **Consistency:** Use same layout for similar content types
2. **White Space:** Don't overcrowd slides
3. **Visual Hierarchy:** Largest = most important
4. **Alignment:** Keep elements aligned for professional look
5. **Contrast:** Ensure text is readable on backgrounds

### Animation (Optional)
Add subtle animations in Canva:
- **Slide transitions:** Fade or slide
- **Element animations:** Rise or fade for text
- **Keep it professional:** Don't overdo animations
